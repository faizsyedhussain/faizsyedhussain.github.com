I'm Faiz, a Social Business Designer based in London.

I believe in a calm approach to business founded on human rights, quality of life and social impact. I have been fortunate to push other founders to think about the impact in their example and draw from disparate disciplines to implement their vision.

I'm currently focussed on Corporate Intrapreneurship in a unique venture environment, with the opposite conditions of a startup or venture studio.

As an entrepreneur, I pivoted ailing venture-funded startups to sustainable business models focussed on their users. Before that, I created a smart textile wearable, aerosol bitters for cocktails, a literary magazine, a writer's guild and a French language school for adult learners.

I speak twelve languages and have served clients in ten timezones.
